This not only saves time and labor for the end user but offers superior performance, durability and leak-proof joint.

Complete Range of SWR Plumbing System

Ajay Drainline offers a complete range of ring fit and Solvent-fit Soil, Waste and Rainwater Plumbing Systems that are suitable for commercial, industrial, housing and public sector developments. These systems are available in 25,32, 40, 50, 63, 75, 90, 110, 140 and 160 and 200mm in both ring and solvent type.

Cost Effective

Ajay Drainline Plumbing system is lightweight and thus reduces transporting, handling and installation cost. The maintenance-free system ensures low lifetime cost.

Corrosion Resistant

Ajay Drainline is corrosion resistant – there is no scaling or pitting. It remains unaffected by the chlorine in the water. Ajay Drainline pipes and fittings do not corrode or rust. It has excellent resistance to any build-up deposits in the system interiors.

Leak Proof

Ajay Drainline system does not require any solvent welding, soldering or threading for making the joints and is pressure tested for 100% leak proof performance.
